# Product Guidelines

## Design Principles
- **Modern & Vibrant Aesthetics:** Use a bold color palette of Slate and Safety Orange to evoke a sense of industry, reliability, and modern energy.
- **Speed & Efficiency:** Interfaces must be optimized for rapid data entry and navigation, catering to busy professionals.
- **Guided Workflows:** Implement intuitive, step-by-step processes for complex tasks like budget generation to ensure accuracy and ease of use.

## Technical Standards
- **Component Library:** Utilize **Shadcn UI** for high-quality, accessible, and customizable interface components.
- **Styling:** Adhere to **Tailwind CSS** for all styling, ensuring a consistent and responsive design across the dashboard.

## Communication Style
- **Tone:** **Professional & Direct.** All application copy should be clear, concise, and focused on business utility. Avoid unnecessary filler or overly casual language.
- **Terminology:** Use industry-standard terms relevant to construction, auto repair, and service providers (e.g., "Estimate," "Quote," "Line Item," "Labor Cost").

## User Experience
- **Mobile Responsiveness:** Ensure the dashboard is fully functional on mobile devices, as service providers may access it from job sites.
- **Accessibility:** While prioritizing speed, maintain standard accessibility practices to ensure a broad range of users can navigate the platform effectively.
